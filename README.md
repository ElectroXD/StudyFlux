# StudyFlux

**StudyFlux** is a modern collaborative platform for students to organize tasks, track syllabus progress, and work together efficiently. It helps streamline group studies, manage daily/weekly/monthly tasks, and stay on top of coursework.

---

## Features

- **Group Study Rooms:** Collaborate with classmates in dedicated study spaces.
- **Task Management:** Track daily, weekly, and monthly tasks easily.
- **Syllabus Tracker:** Stay on top of your syllabus and course deadlines.
- **Worklists:** Create and share task lists with peers.
- **Progress Monitoring:** Visualize progress and completion status.
- **Notifications & Reminders:** Never miss a task or study session.
